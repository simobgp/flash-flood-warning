Turn Around, Don't Drown. Over half of flash flood fatalities occur in vehicles. If you encounter a flooded road, do not attempt to cross. Just 6 inches of water can stall your engine.

A Flash Flood Watch means conditions are favorable for flooding. A Flash Flood Warning means flooding is imminent or already occurring. Move to higher ground immediately.

In cities like New York, Chicago, and Houston, storm drains can quickly overflow. Avoid subways and basement units during extreme rain events.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get flash flood alerts on my phone?

Ensure "Wireless Emergency Alerts" (WEA) are enabled in your smartphone settings. Our map also provides real-time updates directly from the National Weather Service.

What is the most dangerous part of a flash flood?

The speed of the water. Flash floods can happen within minutes of heavy rain, even if it isn't raining in your immediate area.
